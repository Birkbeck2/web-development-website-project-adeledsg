At the chocolate shop

This short video shows a rotating pile of filled chocolates in a local chocolate shop -known as a chocolaterie. Shaped in the form of seashells, made from a mix of white and dark chocolate, and filled with praline -a nut and sugar paste- these chocolates are emblematic of Belgian chocolate.
